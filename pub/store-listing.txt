Full description:
This application adds Bike Share Toronto stations information to MonTransit.

This app contains the bike stations position & availability as well as the latest news from bikesharetoronto.com and @BikeShareTO on Twitter.

Bike Share Toronto is available in Toronto, ON, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display bike stations information (number of available bikes & docks).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from a web-service provided by Bike Share Toronto: 
http://www.bikesharetoronto.com/data/stations/bikeStations.xml

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-toronto-share-bike-android/

This application is not related with the Bike Share Toronto.

Permissions:
- Other: required to load bike stations information and to read news from bikesharetoronto.com and Twitter.
